SIDE PROFILE + TOP-DOWN · TRUE 1:1 SCALE

Top-down — view from directly above (fit to data)

Side view — height vs carry (height exaggerated)

The top-down view is true 1:1 — one yard sideways equals one yard downrange, matching the 3D viewer, so the spread you see is the real spread. (The side view exaggerates height for readability.) Solid line = mean flight path; faint lines = individual shots; solid dot = landing point; dashed ellipse = 1σ dispersion. Pinch to zoom (or scroll on desktop) and drag to pan; double-tap or reset zoom to fit. Tap a landing dot for that shot's full ball data.
