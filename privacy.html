ABSHERMETRICS — Privacy Policy

Effective June 9, 2026

AbsherMetrics ("the app") lets golfers store and analyze their launch-monitor shot data. This policy explains what we collect, how it's used, and how to delete it.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address and a password (stored securely, hashed, by our authentication provider).
  • Golf data you add: the shot and session data you upload or enter (ball speed, launch angle, spin, carry, etc.) and your club settings (lofts, in-bag).
  • We do not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or device sensors, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

How your data is used

  • To provide the app: showing your bag, computing ball-flight, dispersion, and trends from your shots.
  • Your data is private to your account. Other users cannot see it (enforced by database row-level security).
  • We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing.

Where it's stored

Account and shot data are stored with Supabase (a hosted Postgres database and authentication service) on our behalf. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

Account & data deletion

Delete in the app: open Settings (the gear icon) → Delete account. This permanently and immediately deletes your account and all of your shots, sessions, and club settings. This cannot be undone.

Or request by email: email spencer.absher@gmail.com from your account email address and we will delete your account and data within 30 days.

We retain your data only while your account exists; deleting your account removes it.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions: spencer.absher@gmail.com
